Learn More About Us

Welcome to the National Institute of Film Making! We are a leading institution dedicated to nurturing the creative talents of aspiring filmmakers. With a rich history that spans over three years, we have been empowering students in the field of creative arts under our previous name, Indore Art Academy. Our institute is built on the belief of skill-based learning and providing hands-on experiences that enable our students to thrive in the competitive world of film making.

Mission

Our mission at The National Institute of Film Making is to empower and inspire creative individuals to become skilled and successful filmmakers. We are dedicated to providing a dynamic learning environment that fosters artistic growth, technical proficiency, and a deep understanding of the filmmaking craft. Through hands-on experiences, industry-relevant curriculum, and a supportive community, we strive to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving world of cinema.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution for film education, recognized globally for producing talented and innovative filmmakers. We aim to create a vibrant and inclusive community of aspiring artists, where collaboration, diversity, and creative exploration are celebrated. By promoting a culture of excellence, professionalism, and ethical filmmaking practices, we aspire to shape the future of the film industry by nurturing a new generation of storytellers who will captivate audiences with their unique voices and perspectives. Our vision is to empower our graduates to make a significant impact in the world of cinema, enriching the art form and inspiring audiences worldwide.

Our Certified Courses

Photography

2.5-Month Certification Course

Master the art of photography with our 2.5-month certification course. Covering composition, lighting, exposure, and post-processing, it's perfect for enthusiasts. Expert guidance and hands-on sessions teach you to capture captivating images that tell powerful stories. Whether you aim for a pro career or want to enhance skills, this course equips you with the knowledge and proficiency needed to excel. Unleash your creativity now.

Cinematography

2.5-Month Certification Course

Unleash your creativity and master cinematography in our 2.5-month course. Led by industry professionals, it covers camera operation, lighting, and visual storytelling for film and TV. With hands-on experience and professional equipment, you'll gain comprehensive skills to excel in this dynamic field. Discover the art and science behind capturing stunning visuals and bringing stories to life through practical exercises.

Enquiry

Editing

2.5-Month Certification Course

Discover the art of editing in our 2.5-month course. Learn to shape raw footage into compelling narratives through non-linear editing software apps and advanced techniques. Explore pacing, rhythm, and visual storytelling for immersive post-production. With expert guidance, work on real-world projects to hone your skills and develop a unique editing style. Unleash the power of post-production and unleash your creativity!

Enquiry

Cinematography + Editing

3.5-Month Certification Course

Embark on a comprehensive 3.5-month course that combines stunning visuals and powerful narratives. Master cinematography, editing, and post-production to excel in filmmaking. Explore camera operation, lighting, and visual storytelling while honing your editing skills. With an immersive learning experience, gain a holistic understanding of the filmmaking process and unleash your creativity to bring your visions to life.
